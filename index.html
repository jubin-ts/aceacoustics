About Us

The GCC's Premier Acoustic Solutions Partner

With a continuously expanding product portfolio, Acemacan offers the widest range of market-leading suspended ceiling, wall panel, and acoustic solutions available in the region. Every product is selected and supplied with the dual objective of superior acoustic performance and outstanding interior aesthetics.

Acemacan FZ LLC is the exclusive authorised partner of both DEGO Ceiling and DAIKEN Corporation in the GCC Region, operating under the ACE ACOUSTICS® brand.
