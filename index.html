Who We Are

About ACE Acoustics

Acemacan FZ LLC is the exclusive authorised partner of both DEGO Ceiling and DAIKEN Corporation in the GCC Region, operating under the ACE ACOUSTICS® brand.

We specialise in delivering comprehensive acoustic ceiling and wall solutions for every type of architectural space — from standard commercial fit-outs to high-specification custom-engineered interior environments.

With a continuously expanding product portfolio, we offer the widest range of market-leading suspended ceiling, wall panel, and acoustic solutions available in the region. Every product is selected and supplied with the dual objective of superior acoustic performance and outstanding interior aesthetics.

Quality Assurance

Materials & Certifications

All products are manufactured from non-combustible, independently tested, and internationally certified materials.

Glass Wool

Recycled glass, high-temperature melted. A2_s1d0. Bacteria & mould-resistant. Closed edges.

Rock Wool

Stone wool (resin-bonded mineral wool). Fireproof A1. NRC 0.85. RH 99%. Volcanic-eruption replication.

Mineral Fibre

Wet-manufactured perlite wool. Fireproof B2. NRC 0.75. For offices, schools, and retail.

Gypsum

Desulphurized gypsum, 100% recyclable. A2 fire class. NRC 0.73. Used in Degotone & DegoSilent.

Wood Fibre

Daiken natural wood-fiber boards. Excellent absorption, eco-friendly ceiling & wall applications.

Composite Hybrid

Balanced absorption + insulation for multifunctional spaces needing both acoustic control & isolation.
