About Us

Building Acoustic Perfection Since Day One

ACE Acoustics, a division of ACEMACAN FZ LLC, is the exclusive GCC partner for two of the world's most respected acoustic brands — DEGO Ceiling from China and DAIKEN Corporation from Japan.

Based in Ras Al Khaimah, UAE, we bring world-class acoustic ceiling solutions to commercial, institutional, and residential projects across the entire Gulf region. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction sets us apart.
